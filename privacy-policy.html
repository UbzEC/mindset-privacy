Privacy Policy

Mindset — Daily Motivation  ·  Effective 24 March 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information Mindset collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. The short version: we collect as little as possible and never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

Mindset does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never sent to our servers:

  • Your saved / favourite quotes
  • Your notification time preference
  • Your daily reading streak count
  • Whether you have completed onboarding

2. Subscription & Payments

Mindset Pro subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat, which may collect anonymous purchase events (e.g. subscription started, cancelled) to power the paywall logic. No personally identifiable information is shared.

3. Push Notifications

If you enable daily notifications, Apple assigns your device a push token. This token is used solely to deliver your chosen daily quote reminder and is not shared with third parties.

4. Analytics

Mindset does not use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any third-party analytics library. We do not track how you use the app.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party services used by Mindset are:

  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — to deliver your daily notification. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — to manage in-app subscriptions. Governed by RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Mindset is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

All app data (favourites, streak, preferences) is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data immediately. There is no account to delete because no account is created.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version.
